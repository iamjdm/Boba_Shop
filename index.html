Find Your Zen, One Bubble at a Time

We're tucked away in a peaceful corner, but it's worth the journey down Garret Bay Road to discover TeaZen today!

  • Premium Milk Teas made with fresh ingredients
  • Mochi, Taiyaki & Asian Pastries
  • Fresh Poké Bowls & Summer Rolls

  • Open Mic & Poetry every second Friday at 7 PM

Events at TeaZen

Every other Friday night at TeaZen is K-Pop Night! Join us from 8 pm for music, dancing, and special boba flavors you won't want to miss!

K-POP NIGHT (BI-WEEKLY FRIDAYS, 8:00 PM)

Jasmine Chen brings the energy of K-Pop to TeaZen! Get ready for an evening of dancing, singing, and celebrating K-Pop culture with themed drinks and games.

ACOUSTIC CHILL NIGHT (ALTERNATE FRIDAYS, 8:00 PM)

Atlas Grey brings soothing acoustic melodies to create the perfect zen atmosphere. Relax with live music, warm lighting, and your favorite boba drink.
